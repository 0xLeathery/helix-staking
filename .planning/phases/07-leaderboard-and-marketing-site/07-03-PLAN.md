  **Section 2: "T-Shares Explained"**
  - Paragraph: T-shares (Time-shares) are the reward units. When you stake, you receive T-shares based on your stake amount, the current share rate, and bonus multipliers.

  **Section 4: "Penalties"**
  - Sub-section "Early Unstake": If you unstake before your lock period ends, you lose a portion of your principal. Minimum penalty: 50%. Penalty decreases as you serve more time.
  - Sub-section "Late Unstake": After your stake matures, you have a 14-day grace period to unstake. After that, penalties increase linearly until 100% loss at 365 days late.
  - Important note styled as a callout box (border-l-4 border-helix-600 bg-zinc-900/50 p-4 rounded-r-lg): "Penalties are redistributed to remaining stakers, rewarding commitment."

  **Section 5: "Big Pay Day"**
  - Paragraph: After the free claim period ends, all unclaimed tokens are distributed proportionally to active stakers based on their T-share-days. This is a one-time bonus event.
