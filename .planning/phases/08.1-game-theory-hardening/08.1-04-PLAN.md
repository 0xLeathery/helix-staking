<objective>
Add BPD transparency events and a governance delay between finalization and sealing. This allows community verification that all eligible stakes were included before the BPD rate is locked.

Purpose: The authority-gated BPD finalization creates a centralization trust assumption. Transparency events + a seal delay provide an observation window for off-chain monitoring to detect incomplete or biased finalization.
Output: BpdBatchFinalized events emitted per batch, 24-hour seal delay enforced on-chain, indexer ingests new events.
</objective>
