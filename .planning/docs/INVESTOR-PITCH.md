## What is HELIX?

HELIX is a **HEX-style staking protocol on Solana** — the first faithful implementation of the proven HEX tokenomics model on the fastest blockchain.

**Think of it as:** Certificate of Deposit (CD) meets crypto, where stakers earn inflation rewards and early unstakers pay penalties to those who stay.

---

## Why HEX Economics Work

HEX on Ethereum has:
- $10B+ in staked value at peak
- 5+ years of proven game theory
- Simple, sticky mechanics

**The model:**
1. Stake tokens for 1-5555 days
2. Earn T-shares (more time = bigger bonus)
3. Receive daily inflation (3.69% annual)
4. Early exit? 50%+ penalty goes to remaining stakers
5. Late exit? Gradual penalty (use it or lose it)

**Why it works:** The longer you stake, the more you earn. The penalties from quitters go to holders. Game theory rewards patience.
